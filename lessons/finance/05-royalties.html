Royalties

Understand how music royalties work.

Royalties are payments you receive when your music is used. Understanding the different types ensures you collect everything you're owed.

Types of Royalties

  • Mechanical: From reproductions (streaming, downloads)
  • Performance: From public plays (radio, venues)
  • Sync: From use in video/film
  • Master: From use of your recordings

Collecting Your Royalties

  • Register with a PRO (ASCAP, BMI, PRS, etc.)
  • Register with a publishing administrator
  • Register your songs properly
  • Keep your catalog data accurate
⚠️

Many artists leave money on the table by not registering their works properly. Set this up correctly from the start.
