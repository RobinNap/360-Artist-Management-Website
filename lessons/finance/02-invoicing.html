Invoicing

Create professional invoices and get paid.

Professional invoicing ensures you get paid on time and maintains your business reputation. Treat it seriously from day one.

Invoice Essentials

  • Your business name and contact info
  • Client details
  • Invoice number and date
  • Description of services
  • Amount due and payment terms
  • Payment methods accepted

Best Practices

  • Invoice immediately after the gig
  • Set clear payment terms (e.g., 14 days)
  • Follow up politely if payment is late
  • Keep records of all invoices
