The Artist Cycle

Navigate the different phases of an artist's career.

Every artist career moves through predictable phases. Understanding where you are helps you focus on the right activities for your current stage.

The Phases

  • Discovery: Building your sound and identity
  • Development: Growing your audience and skills
  • Establishment: Consistent releases and regular gigs
  • Expansion: Bigger venues, wider reach
  • Maturity: Legacy building and mentorship

Phase-Appropriate Activities

Don't try to do everything at once. In the Discovery phase, focus on your music and brand. In Development, focus on building audience. Match your efforts to your phase.
