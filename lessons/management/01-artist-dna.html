Artist DNA

Understand what makes you unique as an artist.

Your Artist DNA is the combination of traits, influences, and characteristics that make you uniquely you. Understanding it helps you make authentic decisions about your career.

Components of Artist DNA

  • Your musical influences and inspirations
  • Your personal story and background
  • Your values and what you stand for
  • Your strengths and unique skills
  • Your goals and ambitions

Why It Matters

When you understand your DNA, decisions become easier. Should you take this collaboration? Does this venue fit your brand? Your DNA guides these choices.

✍️

Exercise

Write down 5 artists who influenced you, 5 personal traits that define you, and 5 goals you want to achieve. The patterns that emerge are your DNA.
