The Fan Journey

Map how fans discover and engage with you.

Fans don't become superfans overnight. They go through a journey from discovering you to becoming loyal supporters. Understanding this journey helps you nurture relationships.

The Journey Stages

  • Awareness: They hear about you for the first time
  • Interest: They check out your music
  • Engagement: They follow you on social media
  • Conversion: They buy tickets, merch, or subscribe
  • Loyalty: They actively promote you to others

Moving Fans Through Stages

Each stage requires different actions. Awareness needs visibility. Interest needs great music. Engagement needs consistent content. Conversion needs calls to action. Loyalty needs community.

✍️

Exercise

Think about your favorite artist. How did you discover them? What made you become a dedicated fan? Apply those insights to your own strategy.
