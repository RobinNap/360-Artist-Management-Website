Signing a Record Deal

Navigate record deal negotiations.

A record deal can accelerate your career—or trap you in a bad contract for years. Understanding what you're signing is crucial.

Types of Deals

  • Traditional: Label funds everything, owns masters
  • Distribution: You fund, they distribute
  • Licensing: You license for a period, retain ownership
  • 360 Deal: Label takes percentage of all income

Key Terms to Understand

  • Advance: Upfront payment (it's a loan!)
  • Royalty rate: Your percentage
  • Term: Contract duration
  • Territory: Where it applies
  • Options: Label's right to extend
⚠️

ALWAYS have a lawyer review any contract before signing. The cost is nothing compared to a bad deal.
