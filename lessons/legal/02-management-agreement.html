Signing a Management Agreement

Evaluate contracts and protect yourself.

A manager can be incredibly valuable, but the wrong deal can cost you dearly. Understand what you're agreeing to.

What Managers Do

  • Career strategy and planning
  • Booking and deal negotiation
  • Day-to-day business management
  • Industry relationship building
  • Team coordination

Contract Considerations

  • Commission rate (typically 15-20%)
  • What income they commission
  • Term length and exit clauses
  • Sunset clauses (post-term commissions)
  • Scope of authority
