Basic Music Rights

Understand copyright and publishing.

Understanding music rights protects your work and ensures you get paid. There are two main copyrights in every recording.

The Two Copyrights

  • Composition: The song itself (lyrics, melody)
  • Master: The specific recording of that song

Who Owns What

As an independent artist, you likely own both. When you sign deals, you may transfer or license some rights. Know what you're giving up.

Protecting Your Rights

  • Copyright exists automatically when you create
  • Registration provides legal benefits
  • Document collaborations in writing
  • Never sign without understanding
