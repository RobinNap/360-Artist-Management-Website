Writing a Newsletter

Build direct relationships with your fans.

Email newsletters let you communicate directly with fans—no algorithm deciding who sees your content. It's one of your most valuable marketing tools.

Newsletter Best Practices

  • Be consistent—set a schedule and stick to it
  • Provide value—don't just ask for things
  • Be personal—write like you're emailing a friend
  • Include a clear call to action
  • Keep it scannable—use headers and short paragraphs

Building Your List

Offer something valuable in exchange for email signups: exclusive content, early access, or behind-the-scenes material.
