Newsworthiness

Learn what makes content worth sharing.

Not everything you do is newsworthy, and that's okay. Understanding what makes something worth covering helps you create moments that get attention.

What Makes Something Newsworthy?

  • Timeliness: It's happening now
  • Significance: It matters to people
  • Uniqueness: It's different or first
  • Human interest: It connects emotionally
  • Relevance: It relates to current trends

Creating Newsworthy Moments

Plan your releases and announcements around moments that maximize impact. A new track is news. A new track that samples a classic hit is bigger news.
