Reaching Out

Master the art of professional outreach.

Cold outreach is part of the game. Whether you're contacting blogs, playlist curators, or promoters, how you reach out matters as much as what you're offering.

Outreach Principles

  • Research first—know who you're contacting
  • Keep it short—busy people skim emails
  • Be specific—what exactly are you asking for?
  • Make it easy—include all needed links
  • Follow up once—then move on
⚠️

Never send mass generic emails. Personalized outreach has dramatically higher success rates.
