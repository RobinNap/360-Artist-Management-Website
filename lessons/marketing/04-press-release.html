Writing a Press Release

Craft releases that get media attention.

A press release announces your news in a format journalists can easily use. Done well, it makes their job easier and increases your chances of coverage.

Press Release Structure

  • Headline: Clear, compelling, newsworthy
  • Lead paragraph: Who, what, when, where, why
  • Body: Details, quotes, context
  • Boilerplate: Standard 'About' paragraph
  • Contact info: How to reach you
