Getting Booked at a Festival

Stand out to festival programmers.

Festival bookings are highly competitive. Programmers receive hundreds of submissions, so standing out requires strategy and patience.

Festival Booking Realities

  • Applications open 6-12 months in advance
  • Lineup is often decided by relationships and buzz
  • Smaller stages are your way in
  • Regional festivals are more accessible than flagship events

Building Festival-Ready Profile

Festivals want artists with momentum. Focus on building buzz through releases, smaller gigs, and social proof before targeting major festivals.
