Find Your Stage

Discover venues that match your style.

Not every venue is right for every artist. Finding venues that match your sound and style increases your chances of success and leads to better performances.

Researching Venues

  • What genres do they typically book?
  • What's the crowd like?
  • What size audiences do they draw?
  • Who else has played there?
  • What's their reputation in the scene?
