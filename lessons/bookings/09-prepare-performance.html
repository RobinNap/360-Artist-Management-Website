Preparing Your Performance

Get ready for showtime.

Preparation separates professionals from amateurs. What you do before the gig affects how you perform during it.

Before the Gig

  • Confirm all details with the venue
  • Prepare and organize your music
  • Check all equipment works
  • Plan your journey with buffer time
  • Rest properly the night before

Day of the Gig

  • Arrive early for soundcheck
  • Meet the sound engineer and venue staff
  • Test your equipment in the booth
  • Check monitor levels
  • Relax and focus before going on
