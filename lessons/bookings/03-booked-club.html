Getting Booked at a Club

Strategies for landing club bookings.

Club bookings are often the foundation of a DJ career. Here's how to approach clubs professionally and increase your chances of getting booked.

The Approach

  • Build a relationship first—become a regular
  • Network with current residents and staff
  • Have your EPK ready before approaching
  • Start with off-peak slots if offered
  • Prove yourself before asking for more

What Bookers Look For

Bookers want artists who draw crowds, are professional to work with, and fit the venue's brand. Demonstrate all three.
