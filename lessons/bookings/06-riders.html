Build Your Riders

Create professional technical and hospitality riders.

Riders communicate your requirements to venues. A professional rider ensures you have what you need to perform well.

Technical Rider

  • Equipment you need (CDJs, mixer model, etc.)
  • Monitor requirements
  • Any special equipment you bring
  • Stage layout preferences

Hospitality Rider

  • Travel arrangements
  • Accommodation needs
  • Food and beverage preferences
  • Guest list requirements
