Welcome Hi there, and welcome to the Artist Academy!

We are thrilled to see you join our community, and we can not wait to see you grow in your journey. The Artist Academy is the heart of our service. We have spoken to an enormous amount industry professionals who together have over more than a hundred years of experience working with artists on all levels. With all that knowledge combined, we have created this course. It is meant not only as a course but also as a knowledge base. That means you can come back here to freshen up your memory about speciﬁc subjects whenever you are done with this course. We advise creating a folder on your computer where you can save your worksheets and notes. We highly encourage you to make notes!

If you have a Premium or VIP subscription, your Career Coach might give you tasks to do with the help of this course.

If you feel like something is unclear, you can always chat with us or contact your Career Coach. If you want to give feedback about the course, you can do so at the end of every chapter. Now let's get started!
