Artist Name

Choose a name that represents your brand and is memorable.

Your artist name is often the first thing people encounter. It should be memorable, represent your brand, and work across all platforms.

What Makes a Good Artist Name?

  • Easy to spell and pronounce
  • Unique enough to search online
  • Available as a domain and social handles
  • Represents your brand identity
  • Sounds good when announced on stage
⚠️

Before committing to a name, search it thoroughly. Check social media, streaming platforms, and trademark databases. You don't want legal issues later.

Testing Your Name

Say your potential name out loud. Imagine a festival announcer saying 'Please welcome to the stage...' followed by your name. Does it work?
