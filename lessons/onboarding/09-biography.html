Biography

Write a compelling story that connects with your audience.

Your biography tells your story. It's used by promoters, journalists, and fans to understand who you are. A good bio creates connection and intrigue.

Three Versions You Need

  • Short (50 words): For social media profiles
  • Medium (150 words): For event listings and quick references
  • Long (300+ words): For press kits and detailed features

What to Include

Start with a hook that grabs attention. Include your genre, notable achievements, unique story elements, and what makes you different. End with what you're working on now.

⚠️

Avoid clichés like 'passionate about music since childhood' or 'genre-defying sound.' Be specific and authentic.
