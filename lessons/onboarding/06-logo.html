Logo

Create a visual symbol that makes you instantly recognizable.

Your logo is a visual shorthand for your brand. It should be simple enough to recognize at a glance, but distinctive enough to be memorable.

  • Consider how it looks on merchandise

Your logo doesn't need to explain what you do. Nike's swoosh doesn't show shoes. Apple's logo isn't a computer. Focus on being memorable.

Where to Use Your Logo

Once you have a logo, use it consistently: social media profiles, music artwork, website, merchandise, press materials, and stage visuals.
