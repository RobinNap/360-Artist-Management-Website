Online Presence

Build and optimize your digital footprint.

Your online presence is often someone's first encounter with you. Every platform should reflect your brand consistently and make it easy for people to find and follow you.

Essential Platforms

  • Streaming: Spotify, Apple Music, SoundCloud
  • Social: Instagram, TikTok, X (Twitter)
  • Video: YouTube
  • Professional: Your own website

Profile Consistency

Use the same profile photo, username, and bio format across platforms. Make it easy for fans to find and recognize you everywhere.
