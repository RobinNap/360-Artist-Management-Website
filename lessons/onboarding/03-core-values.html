Core Values

Discover what makes you stand out from the crowd.

Your core values are the foundation of your brand. They're what you represent as an artist and what makes you different from everyone else.

Discovering Your Uniqueness

Start by observing yourself honestly. What makes you different? What do you care deeply about?

Questions to Ask Yourself

  • What do you do daily that's different from others?
  • What hobbies set you apart?
  • What are your guilty pleasures?
  • Do you have strong opinions you're not afraid to share?
  • Is your way of speaking or dressing unique?
