Press Photos

Get professional photos that work across all platforms.

Professional press photos are essential. They're used by promoters, media, and platforms. Bad photos can lose you opportunities before anyone hears your music.

What You Need

  • At least 3-5 high-quality horizontal shots
  • A few vertical options for Stories/Reels
  • Both color and black & white versions
  • Different crops (full body, headshot, creative)

Working with Photographers

Share your moodboard with your photographer before the shoot. Discuss locations, wardrobe, and the mood you want to capture.
