Press Kit (EPK)

Create a professional kit that opens doors.

An Electronic Press Kit (EPK) is your professional portfolio. It contains everything bookers, journalists, and labels need to know about you in one place.

What to Include

  • Biography (short, medium, and long versions)
  • High-resolution press photos
  • Music samples or streaming links
  • Notable achievements and press coverage
  • Social media statistics
  • Contact information
  • Technical rider (for bookings)

Format Options

Create both a downloadable PDF version and a page on your website. The website version can include embedded music and videos.
