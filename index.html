About This Guide

Knowledge From Years in the Industry

Robin Nap

I'm an IT specialist by day, but for years I lived a double life as a DJ and producer, playing at festivals and hotspots across the globe alongside artists like Afrojack & Oliver Heldens. I studied at the renowned Nederlandse Popacademie, where I learned from industry professionals, and spent countless hours in the studio developing my sound and crafting live sets.

After some years of doubt, I made the difficult decision to step away from my professional music career. The pressure to constantly improve and the challenges of managing epilepsy since 2018 made me realize I was missing the joy that first drew me to music‚Äîthose moments of pure excitement when creating something new alone in my room.

Whether you're a DJ, producer, singer, or instrumentalist, use this as your roadmap to navigate the music industry maze.
